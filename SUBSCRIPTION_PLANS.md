# Improved Subscription Plans

## Freemium Model

### **Free Plan** - $0/month
- 1 store
- Up to 10 products
- 1 category
- Basic WhatsApp checkout
- No promo codes
- No analytics
- Email support
- NodeBN branding

### **Growth Plan** - $19/month
- 3 stores
- Up to 100 products
- Unlimited categories
- Full checkout features (services, promos)
- Basic analytics (views, conversions)
- Priority email support
- Custom store logo
- Remove NodeBN branding

### **Pro Plan** - $49/month
- 10 stores
- Unlimited products & variants
- Advanced analytics (sales reports, customer insights)
- Multi-user access (up to 3 users)
- Custom domain support
- API access for integrations
- Phone & chat support
- Export data
- Automated marketing tools

### **Enterprise Plan** - $149/month
- Unlimited stores & users
- White-label solution
- Custom integrations
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA guarantees
- Advanced security features
- On-premise deployment option

## Additional Features to Consider
- Annual billing discounts (2 months free)
- Add-ons: Extra stores ($5/month each), Advanced analytics ($20/month)
- Trial periods: 14-day free trial for paid plans
- Referral program: Free months for referrals
