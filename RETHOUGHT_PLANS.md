# Rethought Subscription Plans with Enhanced Perks

After rethinking the perks, I've focused on scalability, automation, and seller success. Added modern features like AI insights, automated tools, and integrations. Made limits more generous and included value-adds like training resources.

## 1. **Starter** Plan - $19/month
- **Store Management**: 5 stores, 500 products
- **Analytics**: Basic sales tracking, 30-day reports
- **Automation**: Automated order confirmations, inventory alerts
- **Integrations**: WhatsApp, basic email marketing
- **Support**: Email support, knowledge base access
- **Branding**: Custom logo, basic store themes
- **Perks**: Free onboarding call, monthly seller tips newsletter

## 2. **Professional** Plan - $59/month
- **Store Management**: 25 stores, unlimited products
- **Analytics**: Advanced dashboards, real-time insights, export reports
- **Automation**: AI-powered customer responses, abandoned cart recovery
- **Integrations**: Full API access, social media auto-posting, payment gateways
- **Support**: Priority email + live chat, quarterly business reviews
- **Branding**: White-label options, advanced customization
- **Perks**: Free marketing templates, exclusive webinars, 10% discount on add-ons

## 3. **Enterprise** Plan - $199/month
- **Store Management**: Unlimited stores & products
- **Analytics**: AI-driven predictive analytics, custom reporting
- **Automation**: Full AI assistant for orders, personalized marketing campaigns
- **Integrations**: Unlimited APIs, CRM integrations, multi-channel sales
- **Support**: Dedicated account manager, phone support, 24/7 priority
- **Branding**: Complete white-label, custom domain, co-branded materials
- **Perks**: Free strategy consultation, VIP networking events, revenue share partnerships

## Key Rethinks:
- **Increased Limits**: More stores/products to support growth
- **AI Integration**: Added AI features for 2026 relevance
- **Value-Adds**: Training, networking, discounts to increase perceived value
- **Progressive Unlocks**: Clear escalation from basic to advanced features
- **Focus on Success**: Perks like consultations help sellers grow their businesses

This structure positions your platform as a comprehensive growth partner, not just a tool. Test pricing with A/B tests and gather feedback from beta users.</content>
